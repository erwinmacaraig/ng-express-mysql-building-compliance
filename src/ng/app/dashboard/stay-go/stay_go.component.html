Emergency Evacuation Procedures

Please note these simplified procedures and talking points.

Key Messages:
  • Safety of all occupants always is paramount.
  • Call for assistance
  • Communicate with occupants, guests and contractors to keep them informed.
  • Do not speak to the Media
  • Move to the Assembly Area
Reminders:
  • Update your details on EvacConnect to remain informed.
  • Remember to undertake your training every 6 months.
  • Participate in your building's Evacuation Exercise every year.
